Instructions

There are two traditional Chinese weapons here.

One of them is a big sword and the other is a long whip.

They are very powerful, and they have different usages.

Now, I will show you how to use them.

Whip

As you can see, the color of the whip is changing over time.
And the shape of the whip handle is also changing over time.
Besides, there existed more than 20 sequential, moving joints in the whip. The sword also includes more than 2 joints.

Mouse interaction

Drag

Hint: When you drag the mouse, the position of the whip will change..

Sword

Hint: When you click the button "speed", the speed of rotation of the sword will change

Keyboard interaction

Note: Please press the following keys in given order.

D

When you press the D key, the sword will open.
Warn: Please make sure that the distance between the sword and the sword is far enough, otherwise the hidden weapon will not be able to shoot.

W

When you press the W key, the hidden weapon will be shoot.

S

When you press the S key, the hidden weapon will be recovered.
Warn: Please make sure that the the hidden weapon was recovered successfully, otherwise the sword will not be able to recover

A

When you press the A key, the sword will be recovered.

Mouse interaction

Double click

Hint: When you double click the mouse, the hilt will move to the position of the mouse.
